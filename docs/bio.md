## Biosketch

I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras.

My research focuses on frustrated quantum magnetism and computational approaches to emergent quantum phases, including:

- Projective Symmetry Group (PSG) classification  
- Mean-field Ansätze  
- Static and dynamical structure factors for experimental comparison  
- Pseudo-fermion functional renormalization group  

My broader interests include renormalization-group methods, Projective Symmetry Group (PSG) techniques, and numerical approaches to strongly correlated systems.
